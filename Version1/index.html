Ellíder is an advanced Hospital Information System with optional Business Intelligence and Mobility capability designed for hospitals of any bed size.

Ellíder Advanced Hospital Information System provides a state-of-the-art, comprehensive automation solution for hospitals of any bed size. The solution automates all departments – front office, clinical, medical records (using EMR), administrative and inventory functions – of a hospital. Leveraging this foundation will enable the healthcare service provider to build the flexible service processes of today and tomorrow. With its powerful functionalities and adaptable modules, it centralizes all end-to-end processes that are needed to transcend organizational barriers and extend seamless services to the patients.

Ellíder Advanced Hospital Information System is available in three versions – Ellíder Lite, Ellíder Standard, and Ellíder Enterprise.

BUSINESS INTELLIGENCE (BI)

Business intelligence solution provides an edge in extracting insights from data taken from the Hospital Information System. Integrated with Ellíder Advanced Hospital Information System, BI enables the hospital management to identify risk, capitalize on opportunities and gain a deep understanding of the business with reports.

PATIENT CARE MOBILE APP

Patient Care Mobile App is designed for patients to stay connected with the hospital. The application enables the patient to book and cancel appointments, view his/her prescription, consultation summary, investigation results, and so on.
